short bio


I am an Assistant Professor with the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU) since Sep. 2018. Prior to that, I was a Researcher at Visual Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) from 2015 to 2018. I received my dual Ph.D. degrees from Zhejiang University and Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Jinhui Yu and Prof. Pedro V. Sander.
